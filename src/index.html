What is this?

Erasmus+ is the EU's programme to support education, training, youth and sport in Europe. It provides opportunities for over 4 million Europeans to study, train, gain experience, and volunteer abroad.

Why are we doing it?

We want to gather all information about projects and organizations connected with Erasums+. Erasmus+ also aims to promote the sustainable development of its partners in the field of higher education, and contribute to achieving the objectives of the EU Youth Strategy.

Who are we?

We are some guys who want to provide you with easy and comfortable access to every possible opportunity.
